About Me

I specialize in building Generative AI applications with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, Search, and Recommender Systems. Currently, I lead conversation focused machine learning initiatives at Apollo.io, where I develop 0-to-1 sales tech products that drive revenue and innovation.

Previously, I delivered high-impact, consumer-facing projects at Meta, Microsoft, Yelp, and Samsung Research. My work spans diverse domains, including Ads Integrity, Search Engines, Content Understanding, and IoT. At Meta, I developed fraud detection systems that improved Facebook Ads' revenue, while at Microsoft, I enhanced Search through Query Understanding and Natural Language Understanding (NLU). Additionally, I contribute to the AI4A (AI for Accessibility) Grants committee and am a reviewer for MLADS (Microsoft ML and Data Science Conference).

Throughout my career, I've secured multiple patents and published papers in international conferences. I’m passionate about EdTech and MOOCs, and I actively engage with the Coursera and PyTorch communities.

Let's connect if you’re interested in AI, EdTech, or exploring innovative machine-learning solutions!
